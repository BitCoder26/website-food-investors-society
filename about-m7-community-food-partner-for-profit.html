Community Food Partner (For-profit) Application Form

Join The Food Investors Society as a Community Food Partner - a growing network of local, values-driven food businesses working to make healthier, fairer, and more sustainable food choices accessible to everyone.

This membership is open to independent retailers, food producers, markets, cookery schools, and other small enterprises that share our mission to create positive change through good food.

4. Visibility and Recognition (required)

Community Food Partners are part of a visible network of independent businesses helping to build healthier and more sustainable local food environments.

As a member, you will:

  • Be listed in the My Food Neighbourhood directory and on our About Us — Supporters page.
  • Be eligible for good food highlights and community spotlight features.
  • Receive opportunities for joint campaigns and co-promotion with local partners.

8. Membership & Voting Rights (required)

“Community Food Partner (For-Profit) Membership includes one £1 share in The Food Investors Society Ltd, registered with the Financial Conduct Authority as a Community Benefit Society. This share is non-withdrawable and non-transferable. Each member organisation has one vote.”

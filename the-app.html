Is this you?

  • You're eating well - but still relying on foods that are highly processed without realising it.
  • You often feel tired, low, or unfocused, despite trying to eat “healthy.”
  • You find it cheaper and more convenient to grab packaged foods than cook from scratch.
  • You've noticed cooking confidence and communal meals disappearing. Most meals are now eaten alone.
  • You want to eat better but don't know where to start amid confusing food labels and marketing.

The truth:

  • Ultra-processed foods (UPFs) are the main cause of food-related disease - more than sugar, fat, or salt.
  • People who eat more UPFs consume 500 extra calories per day on average.
  • UPFs are linked to poor mental health, biodiversity loss, and 10% of global GDP in hidden costs.
  • 75% of the world's calories come from just six corporations - whose profits depend on keeping unhealthy foods cheap and convenient.

That's why foodXchange exists.

To help you see what's really in your food, discover healthier alternatives, and rebuild confidence in cooking and community.

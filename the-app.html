Explore the app's features

Where healthy choices pay dividents.

FoodX

Shop with insight.
Scan and discover the best-value, least processed foods.

  •  Find the best-priced minimally processed foods
  •  Swap ultra-processed items for healthier alternatives
  •  Filter results by your food preferences
  •  Get notified when favourites go on offer
  •  View detailed product info with one tap
  •  Quickly add items to your shopping list or meal plan
  •  Access data offline and sync later

My List

Spend wisely.
Turn every shopping list into lasting value for your health and wallet.

  •  Create and manage shopping lists with ease
  •  Automatically grouped by cheapest retailer
  •  Sort by store or product category
  •  Check off items as you shop
  •  Share lists and sync offline

Cook - Meal Plan

Cook with strategy.
Build your health portfolio through simple, homemade cooking.

  •  Adopt a preset meal plan or create your own
  •  Plan meals weeks ahead with ease
  •  Convert plans into shopping lists instantly
  •  Share meal plans effortlessly
  •  Access offline and sync when back online

Cook - Recipes & Cooking Hacks

Cook smarter.
Create better meals and lasting health from everyday ingredients.

  •  Discover quick, healthy alternatives to common UPFs
  •  Filter recipes by your available ingredients
  •  View details and add recipes directly to meal plans
  •  Join live cook-along sessions
  •  Import your own recipes automatically

Pantry - My Spaces & Items

Manage your assets.
Track, update and organise your pantry to protect food investments.

  •  Create and manage pantry spaces
  •  View all items within each space
  •  Filter items by expiry status — today, soon, or expired
  •  Add, edit, or update pantry items easily
  •  Automatically update pantry when shopping list items are checked off

Pantry - Waste

Get more from every purchase.
Track expiry and waste to make groceries last longer.

  •  View all expiring items in one place
  •  Track weekly and yearly food wastage with visual charts
  •  See the total monetary value of wasted items
  •  Identify the top contributors to waste

My Community - My Neighbourhood

Invest in your community.
Connect with your community through local food events.

  •  Explore a UK map and zoom into your local area by postcode
  •  Find community food activities and independent outlets nearby
  •  View upcoming local food events directly on the map
  •  Connect with activities, outlets, and events through Groups

My Community - My Groups

Invest in connection.
Build a community that grows through shared ideas and healthier living.

  •  Connect with and support a like-minded, growing community
  •  Browse your groups, trending groups and featured groups
  •  View group details and join instantly
  •  Engage in topics — reply, rate, or flag comments as needed
  •  Create new topics and share your ideas

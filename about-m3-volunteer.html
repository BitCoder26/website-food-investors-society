About & Sign-Up

Give your time. Share your talent. Help us create a healthier, fairer food system - together.

Join our team of changemakers and help build a better food future.

The Food Investors Society is powered by people who care. We're looking for volunteers who can bring their skills, ideas, and energy to help us grow. Whether you're an experienced professional, a student, or simply passionate about food, health, and sustainability, your contribution can make a real difference.

We're especially keen to hear from:
Fundraisers · Social entrepreneurs · Marketeers · Administrators · Communications specialists · Wellness influencers & bloggers · Supportive
public figures · Nutritionists · Chefs · Business lawyers · Data analysts · Developers · AI & Machine Learning experts - and anyone else who could
give us a leg up!

Benefits:

✓ A free £1 membership share, funded by the Society
✓ Recognition as a Volunteer Member of The Food Investors Society
✓ Opportunities to apply your skills to a purpose-driven, national
    initiative
✓ Experience collaborating with an innovative, co-operative social
    enterprise
✓ Invitations to team calls, workshops, and networking events
✓ The satisfaction of helping launch foodXchange

Key Membership Details:

•  Duration: Ongoing (no renewal required)
•  Fees: Free (£1 share capital funded by FIS)
•  Share Capital: Includes a £1 member share
•  Share Status: Non-withdrawable

To protect the integrity of our mission, we ask all volunteers and members to declare any personal, professional, or organisational interests that could reasonably be perceived as influencing their participation in the Society.

Do you have any current or past affiliations that may represent a potential conflict of interest?

4. Volunteer Agreement and Expectations (required)

Volunteering with The Food Investors Society is a collaborative, values-based relationship. Volunteers are not employees, and participation is flexible. However, we ask all Volunteer Members to uphold our values of integrity, inclusivity, and respect, and to represent the Society positively.

Declaration:

“I understand that volunteering is an unpaid role and that my £1 membership share is funded by the Society. I agree to act in line with the Society's mission, values and Rules, maintain confidentiality where required, and communicate clearly if I am unable to continue volunteering.”



5. Data Protection and Consent (required)

“The Food Investors Society Ltd will use your information solely for administering your membership and communicating Society updates. Your details will be stored securely in line with UK GDPR.”



6. Voting & Eligibility Confirmation (required)

“Volunteer Membership includes one £1 share in The Food Investors Society Ltd, registered with the Financial Conduct Authority as a Community Benefit Society. This share is funded by the Society and is non-withdrawable and non-transferable. Each member holds one vote, regardless of membership type.”

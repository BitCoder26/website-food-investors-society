Community Food Partner — Non-Profit

About & Sign-Up

Join a network of local changemakers building fairer food communities.

For community-based food projects, co-ops, and social enterprises
working to make healthy, sustainable food accessible to all.

Benefits:

✓ A free £1 membership share, funded by the Society
✓ Listing on the My Food Neighbourhood directory,
    connecting you with local audiences and supporters
✓ Opportunities to collaborate on food literacy,
    skills, and community wellbeing initiatives
✓ Access to Society resources and partnership opportunities

Key Membership Details:

•  Duration: 1 year (annual renewal)
•  Fees: Free (£1 share capital funded by FIS)
•  Share Capital: Includes a £1 member share
•  Share Status: Non-withdrawable

6. Partnership Commitment (required)

“As a Community Food Partner, our organisation shares the values and mission of The Food Investors Society Ltd and supports efforts to build a healthier, fairer, and more sustainable food system. We agree to uphold the Society's values and abide by its Rules as a member organisation.”



7. Data Protection and Communications Consent (required)

“The Food Investors Society Ltd will use your information solely for administering your membership, partnership communications, and Society updates. Data is stored securely in line with UK GDPR.”



8. Membership & Voting Rights (required)

“Community Food Partner Membership includes one £1 share in The Food Investors Society Ltd, funded by the Society. The share is non-withdrawable and non- transferable. Each member organisation holds one vote, represented by its authorised (nominated) representative.”



9. Authorised Signatory Declaration (required)

This section must be completed by the person legally authorised to enter into agreements on behalf of the organisation (may be the same as the nominated representative).

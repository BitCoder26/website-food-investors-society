Our story

The Food Investors Society was born out of a chance meeting of former grocery marketing company colleagues, who recognised their unintentional role in the rise of ultra-processed foods, and committed to work together to promote a healthier food environment. The initial concept drew on the founders' combined expertise: using marketing data techniques traditionally employed in grocery retail to empower consumers in making better food choices, while applying investment strategies from financial institutions to highlight long-term health benefits. This innovative intersection of data, marketing, and finance provided the foundation for foodXchange.
